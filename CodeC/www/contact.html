About Us


Code Crush is an application designed to help students test their knowledge of programming concepts and languages. At Code Crush, we are passionate about empowering aspiring programmers and unlock their full potential. Our programming application is designed to be a versatile and comprehensive tool that nurtures creativity, hones skills, and fosters a vibrant community of learners.


Origin


The company is a team of students of the Polytechnic University of the Philippines taking up Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering who understands and enjoy the challenges and joy of the coding journey.


Mission


Our mission is to provide an inclusive, accessible, and user-friendly platform that enables individuals of all skill levels to learn and practice programming. We believe that coding is more than just writing lines of code; it's a powerful way to solve problems, build innovative solutions, and shape the digital world.
