CodeCrash Manual

Introduction

Welcome to CodeCrush, a fun and interactive quiz game that tests your knowledge of programming concepts. This manual will provide you with all the information you need to get started and make the most out of the app.

Getting Started

To begin playing the game, follow these steps:

  1. Open the app by clicking on the "Play" button on the home page.
  2. Enter your desired username in the provided input field.
  3. Click the "Start Playing" button to begin the quiz.

Game Rules

Here are the rules of the game:

  • You will be presented with a series of multiple-choice questions.
  • Select the answer you think is correct by clicking on the corresponding option.
  • You will earn points for each correct answer.
  • Be quick! You have a limited time to answer each question.
  • The game ends after you have answered a certain number of questions or reached the time limit.

High Scores

Check out the high scores of other players by clicking on the "High Scores" button on the home page. You can see where you stand among the top players.

Review

Want to learn more about programming? Click on the "Review" button on the home page to access video lectures and tutorials on various programming topics.

Conclusion

Now that you have all the information you need, it's time to start your coding journey with CodeCrash. Have fun and good luck!
